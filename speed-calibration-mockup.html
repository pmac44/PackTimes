Calibrate your Speed Factor

Add one or more rides you've actually done. PackTimes will work out your Speed Factor from real data.

How it works. Grab the numbers from Strava, your headunit, or wherever you log rides. Add at least one ride — three across different conditions gives the most accurate result. Heavier loads slow you on climbs and on the flat (panniers add a lot of aero drag), so it helps to vary the load if you can.
